Vegetables Information

Information about Vegetables

Vegetables are edible plants or parts of plants that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are an essential part of a healthy diet.

Types of Vegetables

  • Leafy Greens: Examples include spinach, kale, and lettuce. They are high in iron and vitamins.
  • Root Vegetables: Such as carrots, potatoes, and beets. They provide carbohydrates and nutrients.
  • Cruciferous: Like broccoli, cauliflower, and cabbage. Known for their cancer-fighting properties.
  • Allium: Including onions, garlic, and leeks. They have strong flavors and health benefits.

Eating a variety of vegetables can help maintain good health and prevent diseases.
